Vienna
We're the world's leading sports technology company, at the intersection between sports, media, and betting. More than 1,700 sports federations, media outlets, betting operators, and consumer platforms across 120 countries rely on our know-how and technology to boost their business.
Sport provides people with a common connection - it can bring together and unite people from all walks of life and all corners of the globe to share sporting moments. We are NASDAQ-listed and are one of the fastest-growing sport-tech companies on the planet. Our division, "Sportradar ad:s " provides data-driven marketing and sponsorship services to the iGaming industry, designed to help deliver significant efficiency improvements for marketing investments. The ad:s team develops and provides purpose-built tools and services to meet the individual marketing needs of sportsbooks and media publishers worldwide.
THE ROLE:
<<*>>As a Senior Software Engineer - ad:s at Sportradar<<*>>, you will be tackling all aspects of the software stack, from API design to front-end UI. Become part of a cross-functional team and take on the responsibility of delivering functional excellence and value to the business.
From a technical standpoint, our product consists of multiple microservices that are entirely hosted in a cloud environment and configured with infrastructure-as-code (IaC). Among many AWS services, we use Lambda Functions to run our code, RDS/DocumentDB to store our data and SNS/SQS for messaging.
As an engineering team, we continuously strive to find the most suitable solution for any given challenge. We aim for an appropriate level of abstraction and refactor sensibly, which allows us to maintain an extendable code base. Testing practices help us shape our change and ensure the system behaves as expected.
If you're a software engineer who is passionate about working with cutting-edge technologies and building scalable, resilient systems, developing our product offers a great opportunity to do just that. We firmly believe that people matter and are committed to fostering a collaborative and innovative work environment, where our engineers can grow and learn from each other.
If you think you would be a good fit for our team, we would love to hear from you.
